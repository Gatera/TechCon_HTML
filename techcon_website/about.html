History

Tech Con began in 2010 with a mission to bring together the brightest minds in technology to foster innovation and collaboration. Over the years, it has grown from a small gathering of tech enthusiasts to a globally recognized event, attracting thousands of participants from various industries. Each year, Tech Con has showcased groundbreaking technologies, hosted influential speakers, and provided a platform for emerging startups to shine. Our commitment to advancing technology and creating meaningful connections has made Tech Con a cornerstone of the tech community. As we celebrate over a decade of excellence, we continue to push the boundaries of what’s possible and inspire the next generation of tech leaders.

Mission

At Tech Con, we are driven by a commitment to innovation, education, and community. Our primary goals are:

  1. Foster Innovation: We aim to be a catalyst for new ideas and technological advancements by providing a platform for the latest innovations and emerging trends in the tech industry.
  2. Educate and Inspire: Through our diverse range of keynote speeches, panel discussions, and hands-on workshops, we strive to educate and inspire attendees, equipping them with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in their fields.
  3. Promote Collaboration: We believe that the best innovations come from collaboration. Tech Con creates opportunities for networking and partnership, bringing together professionals from various sectors to exchange ideas and work on shared challenges.

Past Speakers

Dr. Alice Johnson

Chief AI Scientist at InnovateTech

Dr. Alice Johnson is a pioneering figure in the field of artificial intelligence. With over 20 years of experience, she has led groundbreaking research in machine learning and neural networks. Her work has significantly advanced the capabilities of AI in various applications, including healthcare, finance, and autonomous systems. Dr. Johnson is a frequent keynote speaker and has published numerous papers in top-tier journals. At Tech Con 2022, she delivered an inspiring talk on the future of ethical AI.

Raj Patel

Founder and CEO of GreenTech Solutions

Raj Patel is a visionary entrepreneur and the driving force behind GreenTech Solutions, a company dedicated to developing sustainable technologies. Under his leadership, GreenTech has introduced innovative products that reduce environmental impact and promote energy efficiency. Raj’s contributions to the tech industry have been recognized with multiple awards, and he is a passionate advocate for green technology. He shared his insights on sustainable innovation at Tech Con 2021.

Elena Martinez

CTO of QuantumLeap Technologies

Elena Martinez is a leading expert in quantum computing, with a career spanning over 15 years. As the CTO of QuantumLeap Technologies, she has spearheaded the development of cutting-edge quantum algorithms and hardware. Her work has the potential to revolutionize fields such as cryptography, material science, and complex simulations. Elena’s presentations at Tech Con 2020 and 2023 highlighted the transformative impact of quantum computing on the tech landscape.
